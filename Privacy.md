# Privacy Policy

AIS Weather is designed to be privacy-friendly.

## Data We Use

- Approximate or precise location, only when you grant Android location permission.
- Saved city names and coordinates, stored locally on your device.
- Weather settings such as units, theme, refresh interval, and background style.

## How Location Is Used

Location is used only to request weather for your current area while you use the app. AIS Weather does not request background location access. The app does not require an account and does not sell personal data.

## Weather Data

Weather data is provided by Open-Meteo through the AIS Weather Cloudflare Worker proxy. The proxy normalizes and caches weather responses to reduce API usage.

## Advertising

Ads are disabled by default in the current public configuration. The app includes advertising SDK infrastructure so ads can be enabled later through remote configuration. If ads are enabled, supported ad providers may process advertising-related data according to their own policies and any required consent flow.

## Accounts

No account is required.

## Data Sharing

AIS Weather does not sell user data. Weather requests are sent only as needed to provide forecasts.

## Attribution

Weather data provided by Open-Meteo.com.
