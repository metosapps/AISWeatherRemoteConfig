AIS Weather Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

AIS Weather is designed to be a privacy-friendly weather app. The app does not require an account and does not sell personal data.

Data We Use

  • Approximate or precise location, only when you grant Android location permission.
  • Saved city names and coordinates, stored locally on your device.
  • Weather settings such as units, theme, refresh interval, language, notifications, analytics preference, and background style.
  • Basic anonymous app events when usage analytics is enabled.
  • Advertising and device information processed by advertising SDKs when ads are enabled.

How Location Is Used

Location is used only to request weather for your current area while you use the app. AIS Weather does not request background location access.

Weather Data

Weather data is provided by Open-Meteo through the AIS Weather Cloudflare Worker proxy. The proxy normalizes and caches weather, air quality, and city search responses to reduce API usage.

Radar Data

Radar and map views may use RainViewer, Carto, and OpenStreetMap data. These providers may receive standard network request information required to load map and radar tiles.

Usage Analytics

AIS Weather can send basic anonymous app events to Firebase Analytics and the AIS Weather Cloudflare Worker to help improve reliability and product decisions. Events may include screen names, app version, Android SDK version, setting changes, weather refresh events, and broad weather codes.

Analytics events do not include account data, city names, precise coordinates, contacts, or advertising identifiers. Users can turn usage analytics off from Settings.

Advertising

The app can show ads through remote configuration. Supported ad providers may process advertising-related data according to their own policies and any required consent flow. Premium users should not see ads in production builds.

Accounts

No account is required. AIS Weather does not provide account creation, so there is no account deletion flow.

Data Sharing

AIS Weather does not sell user data. Weather requests are sent only as needed to provide forecasts. Analytics and advertising data may be processed by Firebase, Google AdMob, Yandex Ads, Cloudflare, and related service providers as described above.

Data Retention

Saved cities and settings remain on your device until you change them, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Server logs and analytics are retained only as needed for reliability, abuse prevention, and product improvement.

Security

AIS Weather uses HTTPS for network requests. Local app settings and saved cities are stored on your Android device using platform storage.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@metosapps.com.

Attribution

Weather data provided by Open-Meteo. Radar data provided by RainViewer. Map data and tiles may be provided by Carto and OpenStreetMap contributors.
